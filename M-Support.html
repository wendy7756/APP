About Manifestly

Manifestly is a cozy journaling and self-growth app that helps you practice gratitude, affirmations, vision boards, and identity cards — all stored safely on your device (and optionally synced with your personal iCloud account).

Need Help?

  • If the app is not working as expected, try restarting it or reinstalling.
  • If you purchased Premium and features are not unlocked, please make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID, then use “Restore Purchases” in Settings.
  • If iCloud sync is not working, ensure iCloud is enabled in both iOS Settings and inside the app.
